Rules

Rules and etiquette for campers

Set up camp in the designated area.

n the national park or various camping spots He will neatly divide the zones for setting up tents, such as camping grounds for people sleeping in tents. Camping grounds for motorhomes or campervans, but there are many places for us to choose. Take note of where you should go camping. Don't break the rules to go camping in a forbidden place.

Don't camp under big trees.

The area under a large tree with spreading leaves. Looks shady, helps to shade well from the sun. Probably a good location to set up a tent. But maybe you should be careful. The more you plan to go camping during a gale force It can be dangerous to pitch a tent under a big tree. Because when the thunderstorm The branches may break down. Or a tree toppling your tent without knowing it can harm you and the people camping with you.

beware of fire

When camping, it needs to light a campfire for warmth and atmosphere. But that doesn't mean that everywhere will allow you to light a campfire. In case you can make a bonfire If you need heat from the fire to cook or to keep warm When choosing to use the light The fire should be extinguished. Including if you light a lamp The lamp should not be brought into the closed tent.

Chiang Mai, Tent site

Doi Inthanon

Doi Inthanon is the highest peak in Thailand located in Chom Thong District. Chiang Mai Province Originally named "Doi Luang" or "Doi Luang Ang Ka", the name of Doi Inthanon is the name of the king named Phra Chao Inthawichayanon. Ruler of Chiang Mai His Highness was concerned about the northern forests and tried to preserve them. After His Highness Part of the ashes were invited to be enshrined at the stupa on the top of Doi Luang. and changed the name in honor

                    Coordinates

Chiang Mai, Tent site

Doi Luang Chiang Dao

Doi Luang Chiang Dao is a limestone mountain in the Chiang Dao Wildlife Reserve area, Amphoe Chiang Dao. This cone-shaped mountain is 2,195 metres from sea level, which makes it the third highest mountain in Thailand after Doi Inthanon and Doi Pha Hom Pok. Narrow plains on top of the mountain overlook spectacular views that include a sea of fog on the Amphoe Chiang Dao side, Doi Sam Phi Nong, the Chiang Dao mountain range, and the far end of Doi Inthanon.

Nan, Tent site

Doi Phu Kha 

Doi Phu Kha National Park is located in Nan Province along the Luang Prabang Range in North Thailand. The park is famous for its viewpoints along the main road through the park, it's cool weather, the campsites at higher elevations, number of trails and some impressive caves. The nearest city is Nan, the main entrance point to the headquarters area of the park is from Pua Town to the west.

Chet Sao Noi Waterfall 

Seven Little Girls Waterfall It is located in Chet Sao Noi Waterfall National Park. It was declared a national park on December 26, 2016 from National Park. Established in the year 1980, in the past it was under the responsibility of the Royal Forest Department. The origin of the name of Seven Little Girls Waterfall It gets its name from a small village. Located to the north of the waterfall, it's called Ban Sao Noi, sure enough and has explored a small 7-tiered waterfall, so it's named Chet Sao Noi Waterfall.

steps to camping

Let's learn the process of camping.

  • Step1

    should check the weather

    It is very important because it will let you know that the weather is right.
    Is it good for camping? And so that we can prepare the appropriate equipment or clothing.

  • Step2

    Study the camping destination.

    First of all, it's about the distance, how close it is. which we should study the routealways before departure In order to see whether it is appropriate for us or not, because some places It may require expertise in driving and should study the facilities that are available.
    such as toilets or tent sites for the convenience of camping

  • Step 3

    Check equipment before traveling

    Should always check the condition of the device before traveling. that it can be used normally and prepare to complete the required parts The fact that we are willing to waste time checking for sure It's better than wasting time. Solve problems in front of the job for sure Especially the equipment that is very important. The more you have to check to be sure. such as lighting devices or basic first aid kit And one more thing that most people tend to forget is When buying a new device, they often forget to bring it to check or try it out. This may cause problems in use in real situations.

  • Final step

    Get your camping gear ready.

    Prepare the right amount of equipment. You shouldn't bring too many devices.
    because it will cause difficulty in traveling and make it a burden for us whether
    Choosing a tent that fits the number of people Including we should choose high quality equipment.
    Although the price may be a bit high. because these devices are durable and more difficult to damage
